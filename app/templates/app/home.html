What is an FDMS?

A Fiscal Data Management System (FDMS) is an approved software platform that acts as a virtual fiscal device. It connects your software (POS, ERP, accounting) with the tax authority systems (ZIMRA, ZRA) to automatically validate and submit transaction data in real time.
